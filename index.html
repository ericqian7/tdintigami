TDINTigami

What is TDINTigami?

TDINTigami is inspired by Scorigami . TDINTigami displays every unique touchdown to interception ratio by a quarterback since 1932.

Data Collection

Data was collected from profootballreference.com. I used BeautifulSoup to webscrape approx. 2000 webpages. Each quarterback in TDINTigami had to have started at least one game that season and have their position officially be a quarterback. In total there are 3667 quarterbacks included.

Why 1932?

Unfortunately QB data is incomplete pre-1932. Thankfully the quarterback was essentially non-existent back then (there were only 6 unique players at the qb position from 1932-1940!) so the overwhelming majority of quarterbacks are included.
